# Image Sourcing & Licensing Policy (이미지 저작권 정책)

NE Times is a commercial publication. "Found it on Google with the license
filter on" is **not** a usable rights basis: Google's usage-rights filter
relies on self-reported site metadata, CC licenses carry attribution /
no-derivative / non-commercial conditions that are easy to violate, and the
publisher bears liability either way. Therefore:

## Source priority (use the highest available tier)

1. **Licensed stock under company subscription** (Shutterstock, Getty,
   연합뉴스 사진 etc.) — if the team has an account, this is always the
   answer for news photos of real people/events.
2. **Public domain / CC0** — U.S. government works (NASA, NOAA…), Korean
   공공누리 제1유형, Wikimedia Commons items whose license page explicitly
   says PD/CC0 (verify on the file page itself, not the search result).
3. **Permissively licensed with conditions** (CC BY, CC BY-SA) — usable only
   if attribution is printed and (for BY-SA) the use doesn't create a
   derivative-work problem; flag for editor sign-off.
4. **AI-generated illustration** — for concept/topic art only. Never for
   real identifiable people, logos, trademarked characters, or as a depiction
   of a real news event. Mark as "AI-generated illustration" if published.

Never use: random Google Images results, social-media photos, press-agency
photos without a license, movie/TV stills, K-pop idol photos (초상권 +
저작권 double risk).
